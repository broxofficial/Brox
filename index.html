How to buy

Follow these simple steps to join the BROX revolution and become part of our growing community.

1. Create a wallet

Download Phantom or Trust Wallet from the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit phantom.app.

2. Get some SOL

Have Solana (SOL) in your wallet to trade for $BROX. You can get SOL on exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, or directly from your wallet.

3. Go to Raydium or Jupiter

Connect your wallet and swap your SOL for $BROX using the contract address.

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about BROX and our community.

What is BROX? +

BROX is a meme coin built by the community for the community, focusing on transparency and long-term growth.

Yes. Allocation is transparent (70% Community, 20% Airdrop, 9% Burn, 1% Team) and the contract will be renounced.
