How to buy

1. Create a wallet

Download Phantom or Trust Wallet from the App Store or Google Play Store, or visit phantom.app.

2. Get some SOL

Have Solana (SOL) in your wallet to trade for $BROX. You can get SOL on exchanges like Coinbase, Binance, or directly from your wallet.

3. Go to Raydium or Jupiter

Connect your wallet and swap your SOL for $BROX using the contract address.
